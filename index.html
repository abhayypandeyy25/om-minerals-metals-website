Who We Are

Built on a Simple Principle: Earn Trust with Business

Founded in 2019, Om Minerals & Metals is a Government-registered MSME partnership firm headquartered in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, with a manufacturing facility in Bulandshahar. We are a leading manufacturer and supplier of foundry minerals, ferro alloys, refractories, and industrial metals to India's most demanding sectors.

Our commitment to quality and innovation has positioned us as a trusted partner in the defence manufacturing sector. We are a certified vendor to the Ordnance Factory Board and hold NCAGE and Raksha Udymi credentials from the Ministry of Defence.

We are also an authorized dealer for Force Speciality Chem Pvt. Ltd., Haridwar (formerly Forece Polymers Pvt. Ltd.), extending our reach into specialty chemical products for foundry and industrial applications.

Our Mission

To be India's most reliable supply partner for industrial minerals, metals, and refractories — delivering consistent quality, competitive pricing, and unwavering integrity.

Our Vision

To grow into a vertically integrated minerals and metals company with expanded manufacturing — the preferred partner for government and private industries across India.
