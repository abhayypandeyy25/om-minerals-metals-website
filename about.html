Who We Are

A defence-certified MSME from Ghaziabad.

Founded in 2019 as a Government-registered MSME partnership firm, Om Minerals & Metals manufactures and supplies foundry minerals, ferro alloys, refractories and industrial metals. Our manufacturing facility is in Bulandshahar; our admin office is in Ghaziabad's RDC.

Within four years we became a certified vendor to the Ordnance Factory Board, and hold NCAGE and Raksha Udymi credentials from the Ministry of Defence. We supply directly to Central Government Ordnance Factories, Indian Railways, and India's steel and foundry sector.

We are also an authorized dealer for Force Speciality Chem Pvt. Ltd., Haridwar (formerly Forece Polymers Pvt. Ltd.), which extends our reach into specialty chemical products for foundry and industrial applications.

Mission

To be India's most reliable supply partner for industrial minerals, metals and refractories — delivering consistent quality, competitive pricing and unwavering integrity.

Vision

To grow into a vertically integrated minerals and metals company — the preferred partner for government and private industries across India.
